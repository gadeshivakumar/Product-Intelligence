# Product Intelligence Platform

A full-stack product intelligence platform that helps analyze product listings, validate quality issues, monitor competitor pricing, and manage product workflows.

## Live Links

Frontend:
https://product-intelligence-silk.vercel.app

Backend:
https://product-intelligence-kqfa.onrender.com

---

## Features

* Product video upload
* CSV product upload
* Product quality validation
* AI title enhancement
* Competitor pricing management
* Alerts dashboard
* Jobs monitoring
* Pricing intelligence dashboard
* Product editing & revalidation

---

## Tech Stack

### Frontend

* React.js
* Tailwind CSS
* Axios
* Recharts

### Backend

* Node.js
* Express.js
* Prisma ORM

### Database

* PostgreSQL (Neon)
